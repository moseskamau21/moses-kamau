B-FIT GYM Manager

Final Year Capstone Project | Mobile-First Gym Management System

The Problem

In January 2023, I witnessed a payment dispute at my local gym in Nyeri. A member insisted he'd paid his fees, but the manager's handwritten ledger showed no record. The argument lasted half an hour, frustrating everyone. That moment crystallized the problem: gyms were relying on paper records that could be lost, misread, or disputed. They needed a digital solution, but existing international software was too expensive and not designed for Kenyan realities.

The Solution

B-FIT GYM Manager is a mobile-first web application specifically designed for Kenyan fitness centers. It automates the tedious manual processes while respecting local constraints and opportunities.

Key Features

Member Management

  • Digital registration and profiles
  • Subscription plan tracking
  • Automated expiry notifications
  • Member history and analytics

M-Pesa Integration

  • Direct mobile money payments
  • Automatic payment recording
  • Digital receipts via SMS
  • Real-time transaction updates

QR Code Attendance

  • Fast check-in/check-out
  • No expensive hardware needed
  • Works on any smartphone
  • Real-time attendance tracking

Admin Dashboard

  • Business analytics and insights
  • Revenue tracking and reports
  • Member activity monitoring
  • Inventory management

Merchandise Shop

  • Product catalog management

  • Inventory alerts
  • Revenue integration

Feedback System

  • Member ratings and reviews
  • Service quality tracking
  • Trainer evaluations
  • Improvement insights

Technical Stack

React.js Node.js Express.js JSON Storage M-Pesa Daraja API QR Code Generation RESTful APIs Responsive Design

Design Principles

Mobile-First: Designed for smartphones because that's how Kenyans access technology. The interface works perfectly on a 5-inch screen.

Affordable: Uses QR codes instead of expensive biometric scanners. Uses JSON storage instead of costly database servers. No subscription fees that small gyms can't afford.

Offline-Friendly: Works with spotty internet connectivity. Data syncs when connection returns. Essential for gyms outside major cities.

Locally Integrated: M-Pesa is the primary payment method, not an afterthought. Built for how Kenyans actually pay for services.

Simple Interface: Designed for varying digital literacy levels. Clear labels, intuitive navigation, minimal jargon.

Development Journey

Building B-FIT taught me more than any classroom lecture. I learned to balance technical ambition with practical constraints. I learned that the best code isn't the cleverest - it's the code that solves real problems for real people. I learned to listen to users and iterate based on their feedback.

The biggest technical challenge was integrating the M-Pesa Daraja API. Documentation was sparse, testing required actual money transactions, and error messages were cryptic. But persistence paid off. When the first successful payment went through and automatically updated the member's subscription status, it felt incredible.

Impact

For Gym Managers: Reduced administrative workload, eliminated payment disputes, gained real-time business insights, and improved revenue tracking.

For Gym Staff: Streamlined check-in processes, easier merchandise management, less time on paperwork, better member service.

For Members: Transparent subscription tracking, convenient M-Pesa payments, fast QR code check-ins, easy feedback submission.
